About

Resplendence is a two-round graphic designing competition for school as well as college students all across the country. Show us your creativity in the first round by submitting yourself made designs on our online portal. If we think that you really have it in you, you will be invited to attend our 2nd and final round in SRM Institute of Technology, and stand a chance to win Graphic Designing tablets. This is an opportunity that you cannot afford to miss, so tell us if we give you a blank sheet, what can you do with it.
